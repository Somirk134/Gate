# Code of Conduct

Gate follows the Contributor Covenant spirit: be respectful, direct, and constructive.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that helps the community:

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Focus criticism on ideas and implementation details.
- Accept responsibility and correct mistakes.
- Prioritize the long-term health of the project.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Public or private intimidation.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Repeated disruption of maintainers, contributors, or users.
- Bad-faith security disclosure or exploit sharing.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block accounts, or restrict participation when needed
to protect the project and community.

Report conduct concerns privately to <15035267995@163.com>.

## Scope

This policy applies in GitHub issues, pull requests, discussions, community spaces, events, and any
project-related communication.
